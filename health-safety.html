Our Health, Safety & Environmental Approach

Group Agecosco Maritime Company adopts a systematic approach to health, safety and environmental management, designed to ensure compliance with legislation and continuous improvement in performance.

Our Commitments:

  • Protect staff, customers, suppliers and third parties from health hazards and risks
  • Promote awareness of safe practices, believing all accidents are preventable
  • Promote individual responsibility for accident prevention
  • Analyze accidents and incidents with significant consequences
  • Investigate and report all HSE incidents and near-misses
  • Set improvement targets with continuous evaluation and reporting
