Our Survey Specializations

Expert survey services across multiple maritime and technical domains

Cargo Surveys

Comprehensive cargo assessment services ensuring the integrity and condition of goods throughout transportation.

  • Damage Surveys: assessment of soft and hard commodities
  • Lashing & Securing: recommendations and supervision
  • Condition Assessment: pre-loading and discharge checks

Nautical Surveys

Specialized nautical services covering maritime incidents, environmental issues, and vessel-related assessments.

  • Structural Damage: fixed and floating maritime objects
  • Collision Investigation: collision and salvage supervision
  • Environmental Response: oil pollution investigations

Technical Surveys

Advanced technical surveys for complex machinery, vessels, and specialized equipment.

  • Hull & Machinery: vessel hull and machinery surveys
  • Aircraft Engines: engine assessment and evaluation
  • Mechanical Equipment: industrial equipment surveys
  • Crane Systems: lifting equipment assessment
