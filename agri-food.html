Agrifood Sector Services

Comprehensive agricultural commodity inspection and management services by GAFTA-approved professionals across West Africa.

Specialized Agrifood Sector Expertise

Customs and Port Operations Management

Our qualified personnel expertly manage all customs and port operations for vessels carrying agricultural products including wheat, flour, oilseeds, rice, sugar, and other commodities. We coordinate seamless operations for both export and import activities, ensuring efficient delivery of goods to their final destination via truck or rail transport.

Damage Surveys and Legal Coordination

In cases of damages or disputes, our experienced team performs comprehensive surveys on agricultural goods and provides expert legal coordination to safeguard the interests of all entitled parties. Our neutral approach ensures fair resolution of claims.

Professional Sampling Services

For many years, we have provided specialized sampling services to various members of the agrifood supply chain, including feed producers, traders, and importers. Our services focus primarily on cereals and meals, delivered through our qualified personnel stationed at port terminals using state-of-the-art automatic sampling probes.

Independence and International Standards

We maintain the utmost attention to ensure complete independence and neutrality of our services from all parties involved in the supply chain. Our sampling procedures strictly follow national and international contract standards, guaranteeing unbiased and reliable results.

GAFTA Membership and Compliance

As a proud member of GAFTA, we offer comprehensive sampling services during vessel loading and unloading operations, ensuring full compliance with international sampling and reduction standards.
